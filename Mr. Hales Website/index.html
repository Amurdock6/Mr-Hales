WELCOME TO RIVERTON

At Rvierton we have many career opportunites here at the school. The Auto industry is growing and here at Riverton we have classes that can help you get into the industry or help you learn basic car needs, like how to change a tire or your oil.


COMPETITIONS

In the auto motive class, students will be able to compete in tournaments that will have the chance to be placed in Region, State and National levels.

Over the years we have sent students to tournaments and have been very successful in them. We have acheieved 1st in state and at regions multiple times allowing us to send our students to compete at national levles where they can get scholarships to universities.
